The Bet

Andrikanich vs. Schumacher

  • Take 2:
    On September 9, 2010, Greg Schumacher and Chris Andrikanich agreed upon the following bet:
  • The Bet:
    For the Fall Volleyball Session starting September 16, 2010, the person with the most Schumachers at the end of the regular season will be declared the loser.
  • Stakes:
    Loser shall bring pizza and a 6-pack of winner's choice of beverage to winner's house, for an afternoon of Madden Football play.
  • Schumacher:
    A Schumacher is defined as hitting a ball, clearly set to you at the net, into the net, and not going over.
  • Mediator:
    Allie Perrett has been agreed upon as the mediator of all disputed calls.
  • Condition:
    For Schumachers to count, both Chris and Greg must be present.
